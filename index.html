About CampusCart

CampusCart is a secure, student-only web platform designed exclusively for college students to buy and sell academic essentials — lab coats, calculators, textbooks, and more. Our mission is to promote reuse of resources, affordability, and safe on-campus transactions.

Secure Campus Marketplace

Login restricted to @pccoepune.org email addresses. Safe and verified student community only.

Student Community

Connect with fellow students to buy and sell academic essentials within the campus.

Fast Buying & Selling

Trusted College Network

Built by students, for students. Promoting reuse and sustainability in education.

Our Objectives

  • Provide a secure student-only marketplace
  • Promote reuse of academic resources
  • Simplify buying and selling within campus
  • Ensure safe communication through college email IDs

Security First
